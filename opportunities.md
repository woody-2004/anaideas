# Opportunities Log

Running cumulative list of all AI-leveraged business opportunities discovered. Each entry is logged once; do not duplicate. New opportunities are appended in dated batches. Scores use a 1–10 scale per dimension (higher = more favorable in all columns, including Startup Cost and Competition, which are inverted so "10 = cheap/low-competition"). Composite = simple average of the 7 dimensions, ×10, rounded.

Legend: **SC** = Startup Cost (10=cheap), **TTR** = Time to First Revenue (10=days), **Comp** = Competition (10=low competition), **Scale** = Scalability Ceiling (10=$100k/mo+ achievable), **Like** = Likelihood of Success (10=high confidence), **AI-Lev** = AI Leverage (10=AI massively cuts cost/time/headcount), **CAS** = Customer Acquisition Speed (10=days/weeks).
