## Objection handling notes (for live calls)
- **"We already have someone answering phones."** — Great, this isn't a replacement, it's overflow/after-hours coverage so you never lose the calls your team can't get to. Most clients keep their existing staff and just stop losing the missed-call leads.
- **"Will customers know it's AI?"** — It's transparent and customers can always ask for a human; the focus is speed of first response, not deception.
- **"How much does it cost?"** — Pilot pricing $300–500/mo for 30 days, standard $500–1,500/mo after, scaled to call volume. Compare to even one missed $3,000–8,000 job.
